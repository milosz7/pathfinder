How to use

Drawing the path

Click on a cell to select it. If you change your mind click on an active cell to unstage it.

Selecting start/end points

Double click on an active cell to make it a start/end point. Double click again to unstage it.

Shortest path generation

After selecting the points click the "compute" button. The pathfinder will show you the shortest route!
